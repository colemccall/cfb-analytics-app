EDGE — Efficiency-Driven Grade per Event

Our custom metric. Crunch-time plays count 2×. Garbage-time plays count 0.25×. Every play is opponent-adjusted using SP+ ratings. The result is a rating that reflects what a player actually did when it mattered — not just how many yards they accumulated.
